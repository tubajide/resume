Introduction

My name is Babajide Akinshola Israel. I am a software developer with experience on building web and mobile application using different languages. I am also a UI/UX designer
I enjoy developing softwares to make work simpler, easier, faster and stress free with my skills.

I can work with you to create a solution that attract attendtion, generate meaniful converation and build fans

I am a product designer. My job is to lead research for a new consumer financial product. My process is a human-centered approach to (re)designing and building new technology. I believe in making useful things for people versus building shiny new things. My superpower is to listen to how people feel, observe how someone uses something, patterns and habits they have formed, and from there piece together what might make the task a bit easier, their goals a bit more attainable, and ultimately meet their unmet needs.

Always down to collaborate

I love listening to people’s stories. Getting to know people on a deeper level,
to understand how they choose to move through life. I want to get at the moment that changed everything. I want to understand the why behind decisions, in order to unearth the need behind the top soil. Selfishly,
it helps me learn about myself and to gather perspectives unlike my own.
I love to problem solve. I want to prototype near-term solutions to be tested, and simultaneously investigating long-term solutions. I want to go away from the problem and come back with fresh eyes. There is always more than 100 ways to solve the same problem—perhaps it’s not even a problem…
